George Brown College

George Brown College (GBC) is a public college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Established in 1967, the college offers a wide range of programs in business, technology, health sciences, hospitality, and design.

Programs and Faculties:
School of Computer Technology
School of Business
Centre for Health Sciences
School of Hospitality & Culinary Arts
School of Design
Campus Locations:
Casa Loma Campus – Focuses on technology and skilled trades programs.
St. James Campus – Offers business, design, and culinary arts programs.
Waterfront Campus – Specializes in health sciences and nursing programs.
Why Choose George Brown College?

✅ Strong industry connections for co-op and internships.
✅ Hands-on, practical learning with state-of-the-art facilities.
✅ Located in the heart of downtown Toronto, providing access to job opportunities.

George Brown College is known for its emphasis on real-world experience, helping students build the skills needed for successful careers.
